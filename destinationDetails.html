SATPARA LAKE

Satpara Lake is a serene high-altitude lake located near Skardu, Pakistan, surrounded by the majestic peaks of the
Karakoram Range. It is a popular destination for tourists seeking peace and tranquility, as well as adventure enthusiasts
looking for activities like boating, fishing, and hiking. The lake's crystal clear waters and stunning
landscape make it an ideal spot for photography and nature lovers.
